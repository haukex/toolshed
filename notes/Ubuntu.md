My Notes on Ubuntu
==================

If you get `cloud-init` warnings on boot:

    sudo touch /etc/cloud/cloud-init.disabled

Or see my notes on removing it completely at
<https://github.com/haukex/raspinotes/blob/main/BaseInstall.md>
